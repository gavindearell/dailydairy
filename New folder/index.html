About Us

Why choose our Product?

Daily Dairy is a food and beverage business that looks to bring smiles of delight to our customers through our crunchy delicious toast-milk. We aim to be a blessing for the people around us. We want to share our happiness and joy by selling our bread-milk at a small and purchasable price for everyone.

Our business would like to mainly produce bread-milk toast for the consumption of our potential customers. Our target demographic for our products would be Millennials and Gen Z consumers with ages ranging from 15 to 35, but we are open for any customers beyond that age range!
